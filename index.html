I'm a full-stack developer based in the East Gippsland region of Victoria, Australia.

Here's some projects I've worked on so far

Regional Mental Health Australia (RMHA) Inc.

This is a not-for-profit I established in 2020 to raise funds and support mental health initiatives, focusing on people living in rural and regional Australia. Rather than providing clinical treatment directly, RMHA raises money and distributes it to community organisations that deliver mental health services and advocacy.

Mindset Movie Reviews

Mindset Movie Reviews is a movie review project I started in 2024. It is focused on creating and publishing movie reviews as YouTube videos.

Words With Friend Cheat

This site is a free online Words With Friends® word finder and anagram solver. Enter the letters on your rack (specify a prefix, suffix, or letter sequence already on the board) and playable words are generated with their projected scores. It is written in Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
